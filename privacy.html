2. Information We Process

  • Profile information: nickname, birth date, birth time, sex, language, numerology name, and related profile fields
  • Person card information: labels, relationship types, birth details, notes, and related fields
  • Question and verdict history: question text, parsed results, verdicts, oracle outputs, and history records
  • Reflection data: whether you acted, outcome ratings, notes, and verdict accuracy feedback
  • Settings: language, oracle preferences, draw mode preferences, and notification time settings
  • Purchase data: product identifiers, receipts, or transaction data required to verify or restore purchases
  • Support information: emails and information you choose to send to us

3. How We Collect Information

  • Information you directly enter in the app
  • Records and settings created through your use of the app
  • Operating system permission status when you enable notifications
  • Store-provided purchase and restore data

4. Why We Use Information

  • To provide verdicts, oracle interpretations, history, compatibility, and recurring flow features
  • To power reflection, repeated-pattern analysis, and follow-up records
  • To support local storage, encrypted backup export, and restore flows
  • To send local reminders when you enable notifications
  • To verify purchases, grant entitlements, and restore access
  • To handle support, abuse prevention, and service reliability

5. Storage and Retention

Verdicta stores profiles, questions, verdicts, reflections, and settings on your device. This content is not uploaded to an operator server or synchronized to a cloud account.

  • Local data may remain on your device until you delete it or remove the app.
  • Encrypted backup files remain where you save them.
  • Store transaction data is processed by Apple or Google under the relevant store policy; the app stores entitlement status on your device.
  • Support emails are retained by the email provider only as needed to answer the request and meet legal obligations.

6. Sharing and Service Providers

We do not sell personal information. We may use service providers in the following categories:

  • App store payment operators such as Apple App Store and Google Play
  • Email providers used only when you choose to contact support

Profiles, questions, verdicts, and reflection records entered in the app are not sent to these providers.

7. Permissions and Device Features

  • Notifications: optional, used for daily flow reminders and follow-up reminders
  • File access: optional, used for encrypted backup export and import

You can revoke optional permissions at any time in your device settings.

8. Your Choices

  • You can edit or delete profiles, questions, history, and person cards.
  • You can delete all locally stored app data from within the app.
  • You can turn notifications off at any time.
  • You can contact us regarding privacy issues at the email above.

9. Children

Verdicta is not designed for direct use by children where parental consent is required under applicable law.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect service changes, legal requirements, or operational changes.
